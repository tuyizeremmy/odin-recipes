Chicken Wings


These baked whole chicken wings have all the flavor and crispy skin of baked chicken legs, juicy baked chicken thighs, or even regular baked chicken wings combined with the satisfaction of deep fried wings!

You don’t need breading or a vat of oil to make crisp wings… let me show you my easy method that uses pantry staples you probably have on-hand.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb Whole chicken wings
  • 3 tbsp Olive oil (or avocado oil)
  • 2 tsp Sea salt
  • 1 tsp Black pepper
  • 1 tsp Onion powder

Steps to Make chicken Wings

  1. Season the Wings
  2. Bake them for 25 min
  3. Pour Olive Oil
  4. Ready to Eat!
