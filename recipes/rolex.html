Rolex


Ugandan Rolex, commonly referred to as Rolex, is a popular food item in Uganda, combining an egg omelette and vegetables wrapped in a chapati. This single-portion dish is quick to prepare, and can be eaten at any time of the day, from breakfast to a lunch or supper meal or snack

Ingredients

    Chapati
  • 3 C. all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp. Kosher salt
  • 1 tsp. granulated sugar
  • 3 tbsp. oil I used grapeseed oil, but any vegetable oil will work.
  • 1¼ C. water You may use a little more or less depending on the humidity of your area.
    • Omelette
  • 12 eggs divided, 2 eggs per serving
  • 3/4 C. shredded cabbage divided, ~2 tbsp. per serving
  • 6 tsp. Roma tomato finely chopped, 1 tsp. per serving
  • 6 tsp. red onion finely chopped, 1 tsp. per serving
  • 3 tsp. bell pepper finely chopped, 1/2 tsp. per serving
  • ½ tsp. Kosher salt

Steps to Make Chapatti Rolex

  1. make a chapati
  2. make an omelette
  3. put them together
  4. Ready to Eat!
